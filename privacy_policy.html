Privacy Policy for QuickScan (com.quickscan.app)

Last updated: Today

QuickScan does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All scanned QR codes and barcodes are processed on the user's device. Data is not uploaded or shared with any server.

What Data We Store

Scan history is stored only on your device. You may clear it at any time from the app settings.

Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. For details, see Google's privacy policies.
